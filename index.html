1st - 3rd October 2021

It’s onnn

Re-Iterate is a 48 hour redesign marathon, from 1st to 3rd October where we facilitate collaborative possibilities for participants to explore, critique and address real world projects.

Does a weekend full of fun and get to know interesting people sound nice to you? Or are you super interested in design method and how design-thinking can be applied in a process? Then we’re looking for you! All you need to bring is curiosity, motivation to think big and be ready for a lot of fun.

Frequently Asked Questions


Who can attend?

Everyone is welcome! Participation is open to students from around the globe🌻

Do my teammate and I need to register individually?

Nope! One of the members can register and fill out the details of the other participants while confirming their registration.

Do we get prizes?

You bet! All participants stand chances to win cool schwag bags, internships and licenses. Winners get prizes worth upto $200 😉

What is the team size?

Project submissions will be in groups of up to 2 people. If you don’t have a team hop on to our discord server to network with like minded people and form teams.

Is my registration confirmed ?

You would get an email with the registration confirmation after you've completed the process. You may ask reach out on discord for more assistance.

How can I join the speaker session?

All participants will receive the link to the session in the confirmation emails post registering for the event. It would also be streamed live on YouTube.

Still have a query?

Reach out to the organising team via discord or reach out to us via email.
